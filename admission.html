ADMISSION

  • Parents seeking to admit their children in Ideas schooling System should first complete and submit the registration form at the respective branch (the form is attached on the last page of this prospectus).
  • A photocopy of the KMC or Cantonment Board birth certificate needs to be attached to the form.
  • On the basis of performance of the child in the test, along with an interview with the parents, the admission form will be given which has to be duly filled and submitted along with two passport size photographs
    •
 

Admission to Nursery start for the children who have reached the age of 2.5years.
